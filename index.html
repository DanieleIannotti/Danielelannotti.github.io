About

I am a PhD student working on quantum information theory, quantum many-body physics, and quantum gravity.

My research focuses on entanglement, non-stabilizerness (magic), and the structure of quantum states in chaotic systems and holographic theories.

Research

  • Quantum magic and stabilizer entropies
  • Entanglement in many-body systems
  • Quantum chaos
  • Geometry of quantum information
  • AdS/CFT and black hole information
